CC9: Big Data - Extracting a story from millions of prices

I used price data from xxxx, transformed it in xxxcolabxxxx to identify pairs of items that were the same but specified for women or men. I averaged shop prices by region, and then used the output dataset to generate a JSON chart grid. The prices are smoothed using a moving average and restricted until 2006 for comparison. The motivation was common literature on gendered prices or the better-known "Pink tax", although I found no evidence of that for the selected item (leather boots).
